    ABOUT RUGGERFEST 2013

    The 37th Annual Ruggerfest is attracting international teams and the best competition in the United States. Ruggerfest features mens club and premier, women’s club, men and women’s collegiate, old boys, touch and high school rugby. Why play rugby in the cold? Come to sunny Florida for a weekend of rugby and a swim at the beach.

    FEATURING


    • International Touring Side
    • Men's Club Rugby
    • Old Boys Championship
    • Boys & Girls HS Ruggerfest Challenge
    • International Touch Tournament
    • Excellent Rugby Social!
