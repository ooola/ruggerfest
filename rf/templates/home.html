    ABOUT RUGGERFEST 2014

    THE 38TH ANNUAL RUGGERFEST IS ON! We are attracting international teams and the best competition in the United States to meet in sunny south Florida the last weekend in February for our annual pre-season competitive tournament. Ruggerfest features mens club and premier, women’s club, men and women’s collegiate, old boys, touch and high school rugby. Why play rugby in the cold? Come to sunny Florida for a weekend of rugby and a swim at the beach.
