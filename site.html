ABOUT RUGGERFEST 2012

The 36th Annual Ruggerfest is attracting international teams and the best competition in the United States. Ruggerfest features mens club and premier, women’s club, men and women’s collegiate, old boys, touch and high school rugby. Why play rugby in the cold? Come to sunny Florida for a weekend of rugby and a swim at the beach.

FEATURING


  • International Touring Sides
  • Men’s Premiere Exhibition Game
  • HS Exhibition Games
  • International Touch Tournament
  • Best Rugby Social on the East Coast!!!

LOCATION AND TIMES


February 25 and 26th 2012. Central Broward Regional Park. 3700 North West 11th Place, Lauderhill, Florida 33311.
